MAKING VERIFICATION SIMPLE FOR EVERYONE

Secure your certificates using blockchain technology to prevent fraudsters from creating fake documents using your organisation's name.

Individuals can verify the authenticity of your certificate in seconds, increasing overall efficiency while significantly reducing the risk of scams and fraud.

PATENTED VERIFICATION TECHNOLOGY

  • Organisations can now leverage blockchain technology to secure their certificates
  • Document issuers can control who can verify certificates with public and private permissions.
  • Users benefit from quick and easy verification via their mobile device.
  • No coding, existing software or special hardware is req
  • Overall efficiency is improved by eliminating long wait times to verify certificates.

FEATURES AND BENEFITS OF
VERIDOC CERTIFICATE

Build Trust and Confidence

Users can verify that a certificate is authentic and have confidence in your organization.

Blockchain Secured

A unique digital fingerprint of the certificate is stored on the blockchain and can’t be edited or tampered with. Any changes to the original certificate will cause the verification check to fail.

Easy to Verify

Simply scan a QR code on the certificate using a mobile device. If all the technology checks are successful, a verification page will display the original certificate for one final visual comparison check.

Public and Private Certificates

Document issuers can choose to let anyone verify their certificates or utilize access control to manage who can verify their certificates.

Certificate Templates

Certificate templates are fully tailored to your organization and are managed via our web app. No coding or existing software is required.

Easy Certificate Management

Document issuers can disable verification for certificates that are cancelled or expired. A message can be added to alert users who try to scan and verify a cancelled certificate.

USE CASES

Universities & Training Organizations

Secure education certificates with ease. Reduce revenue loss due to fake certificates and students not completing their course. Eliminate time spent in responding to employers who are seeking to verify education qualifications and training certificates.

Medical

Produce medical certificates with ease while maintaining patient privacy. Health records can be verified against encrypted digital hashes on the blockchain to ensure that patient databases haven’t been changed or manipulated.

Sport

Secure sporting certificates such as coloured belt gradings in martial arts, as well as accredited coaching qualifications and licences. This ensures that players are protected from fraudulent coaches as well as sporting clubs who are not doing the right thing.

Building & Construction

Secure building approvals and inspection certificates to ensure that the public is protected and complies to council requirements. By securing builder certificates and licences, people can ensure that a builder is properly qualified to do any construction work.

Professional Development

Secure professional accreditations such as Charted Accountant or Charted Engineer status. Aviation associations can verify the total number of hours that their pilots have spent flying in order to qualify for various pilot licences.

Government

The world relies on government issued certificates such as birth certificates, marriage certificates, citizenship certificates, land titles, and much more. Ensuring that these certificates are authentic and protected from fraudsters will help prevent ID theft amongst a range of other criminal activities.
